Intership

PT. Adiprima Suraprinta

July 2023 - December 2023

During my internship at PT Adiprima Suraprinta, I was rolled in 3 places alternating every month, namely in the Workshop, MCC (Motor Control Center), and GE (general electric). In this workshop, there are 2 divisions, a mechanical division and an electrical division and I am in the electrical division because my major is electro, here is a place to repair various electric motors of various types and sizes, starting from troubleshooting, replacing grease, replacing bearings and disassembling motor parts. MCC / motor control center is part of the electrical division that repairs damage to the panels connected to the motors, here usually troubleshooting panels ranging from checking voltage, checking current, checking contactors and other electronic components in the panel. GE / general electric, this is part of the electrical division that performs troubleshooting on room air conditioners, factory area lights, and operates generators if there is a blackout. That's all I know during my internship at PT Adiprima Suraprinta.
